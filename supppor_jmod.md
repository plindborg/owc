AI Runstreak Companion – Support

AI Runstreak Companion helps you keep your running streak alive with one simple daily rule: complete at least 1 mile (1609 meters) in a single run.

The app gives you:

Daily streak status and timeline
Risk insights based on recovery signals
A “minimum safe effort” recommendation for today
iPhone + Apple Watch sync, including watch complication status
Why we need HealthKit

We use HealthKit to read recovery metrics:

HRV (heart rate variability)
Resting heart rate
Sleep
These signals power your daily risk and recovery insights.
We do not use HealthKit to import your run activities in the current version.

Why we need Strava

We use Strava to import your running activities:

Detect if today includes a qualifying run
Verify the 1-mile (1609 m) single-run streak rule
Keep timeline and Watch status accurate
Without Strava connected, automatic run detection is limited.

Privacy and control

You can disconnect Strava at any time.
Health data access is read-only for supported metrics.
Permissions can be managed in iOS Settings at any time.
