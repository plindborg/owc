## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting started

**How do I log a meal?**
Tap the **+** button at the bottom of the screen. You can search for food by name, scan a barcode, or pick from your recent and favourite foods. Enter the amount and tap Save.

**How do I choose my creature?**
When you first open the app you'll go through a short onboarding where you pick your creature type. Your choice also sets the app icon automatically. You can change the app icon later in **Settings → App Icon**.

**What are evolution stages?**
Your creature levels up as you log meals consistently. There are 10 evolution stages — each one brings a new look for your creature. Keep your streak alive to keep evolving!

### Calorie tracking

**Where does the nutritional data come from?**
KalorKraken uses **Open Food Facts** (openfoodfacts.org), a free, open-source food database with millions of products. If a product is missing or incorrect, you can contribute directly at openfoodfacts.org.

**Can I add a food that isn't in the database?**
Yes. In the food search screen, scroll down and tap **Add custom food**. Enter the name and nutritional values manually. Custom foods are saved locally for future use.

**How is my calorie goal calculated?**
Your goal is based on the information you enter during onboarding (age, height, weight, activity level, and goal). You can update these at any time in **Settings → My Profile**.

**What is a macro split?**
The macro split shows how many of your calories come from protein, carbohydrates, and fat. You can set your own target percentages in **Settings → Nutrition Goals**.

---

### Creature & achievements

**My creature isn't evolving — why?**
Evolution requires consistent logging. Missing several days in a row can pause your progress. Try to log at least one meal per day to keep your streak going.

**How do I unlock achievements?**
Achievements unlock automatically when you meet their criteria — no need to tap anything. You'll see a notification banner when one unlocks. Check your full achievement list under the **Progress** tab.

**Can I change my creature type after onboarding?**
Not currently. The creature type is tied to your profile from the start. This may change in a future update.

---

### Data & privacy

**Is my data stored in the cloud?**
No. All your data — logs, measurements, creature progress — is stored only on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPhone, the data will be included in your regular device backup, handled entirely by Apple.

**Can I export my data?**
Data export is on our roadmap for a future update.

**I accidentally deleted a log entry. Can I recover it?**
Unfortunately not — deletions are permanent. As a workaround, you can re-add the entry manually.

---

### Notifications

**How do I turn off daily reminders?**
Go to **Settings → Notifications** inside the app, or turn them off in **iPhone Settings → KalorKraken → Notifications**.

---

### Troubleshooting

**The barcode scanner isn't working.**
Make sure KalorKraken has permission to use the camera: **iPhone Settings → KalorKraken → Camera → Allow**.

**The app is running slowly or crashing.**
Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, restart your iPhone. If it continues, please contact us (see below) with a description of what happened and which iPhone model you're using.

**A food's nutritional values look wrong.**
The data comes from Open Food Facts and may occasionally contain errors. You can report incorrect entries directly at openfoodfacts.org to help improve the database for everyone.
