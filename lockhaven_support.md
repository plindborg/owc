## About Lockhaven

Lockhaven is a private vault for notes, photos, videos, and documents stored locally on your iPhone.

## FAQ

### What does Lockhaven Pro unlock?

Lockhaven Pro unlocks unlimited files in your vault through a one-time purchase.

### How many files are included for free?

The free version includes up to 10 files.

### How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open the paywall and choose "Återställ tidigare köp".

### Can I reset my PIN if I forget it?

No. Lockhaven is designed so that a forgotten PIN cannot be recovered by the developer. There is no backup code or account-based recovery flow in the current version.

### What is a decoy vault?

A decoy vault is opened with a separate decoy PIN and shows alternate or empty content. It is intended for situations where you need a second unlock path.

### Why did I receive an intrusion notification?

Lockhaven can notify you after an incorrect PIN attempt. If camera permission is enabled, the app may also save an intruder photo in the local intrusion log.

### Why was I asked about deleting original photos?

After importing photos or videos into the encrypted vault, Lockhaven can offer to remove the originals from your photo library. This is optional.

### Does Lockhaven sync my files to the cloud?

Not in the current implementation. Lockhaven is built around local, on-device storage.

## Troubleshooting

### Face ID or Touch ID is not available

Check that:

- biometric authentication is enabled on the device
- you have already set up your PIN
- Lockhaven has permission to use Face ID

### I am not getting intrusion notifications

Check that:

- notifications are allowed for Lockhaven in iOS Settings
- you have completed the permission prompt

### Intruder photo is missing

Check that:

- camera access is allowed for Lockhaven
- the failed PIN attempt happened while the camera was available

## Important Security Notice

Lockhaven is intentionally strict:

- forgotten PINs cannot be recovered
- there is no manual admin unlock
- there is no support-side data recovery for local vault content

## Suggested Support Page Footer

Lockhaven support is provided on a best-effort basis. For security reasons, support cannot recover forgotten PIN codes or unlock protected vault data.
