FAQ
How does the game work?
You build walls to capture territory. If a ball hits a wall while it is still growing, you lose a life. Capture at least 75% to clear the level.

How many levels are in the game?
SplitZone has ongoing progression with increasing difficulty (more balls and higher speed each level).

What is free?
The first 3 levels are free.

How does the subscription work?
After the free levels, SplitZone+ unlocks continued progression. Monthly and yearly plans are available.

How do I restore purchases?
Open the paywall in the app and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your App Store account settings and manage subscriptions there.

The app crashes or feels slow. What should I do?

Restart the app
Update to the latest iOS version
Restart your device
Contact support if the issue continues
