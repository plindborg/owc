## The Short Version

Planner does not collect your personal data. Your tasks, projects, notes, and travel information never leave your device and your iCloud account. We don't have servers that store your content. We don't sell data. We don't track you.

---

## What Data Planner Uses

### Data You Create

Everything you type into Planner — tasks, notes, projects, tags, trips, bookings, and checklists — is **your data**. It is stored:

- **Locally** on your device, in Planner's private app container.
- **In your personal iCloud account**, if you have iCloud sync enabled.

This data is never sent to Lindborg Software or any third party. We have no access to it.

### Calendar & Reminders

If you grant Planner permission to access Apple Calendar, the app reads your calendar events to display them in the Today and Upcoming views. This data is read locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere outside of it.

Reminders scheduled by Planner are created locally through iOS/macOS notification APIs.

Planner does not upload calendar or reminder data to any server.

### Crash Reports & Diagnostics

Planner does **not** include any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs (such as Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Sentry, etc.).

If your device has **Share iPhone/Mac Analytics** enabled in system settings, Apple may collect anonymous crash logs and share them with developers through App Store Connect. This is controlled entirely by Apple and your system settings — Planner does not initiate or configure this.

### iCloud Sync

When iCloud sync is enabled, your Planner data is synced via **Apple CloudKit** to your personal iCloud account. This sync is encrypted by Apple in transit and at rest. Lindborg Software does not have access to your CloudKit data. Apple's privacy policy applies to iCloud storage: [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Permissions

Planner may request the following permissions. All are optional unless stated.

| Permission | Why | Required? |
|---|---|---|
| **Calendar (Full Access)** | To read and display your calendar events alongside tasks, and to create calendar events from tasks | Optional |
| **Notifications** | To deliver task reminders at the time you specify | Optional |
| **iCloud** | To sync your data across your devices | Optional |

You can revoke any permission at any time in your device's Settings app. Revoking a permission does not delete your Planner data.

---

## Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored locally and in your iCloud account. Lindborg Software does not store copies of it.

To delete your data:

1. **Delete the app** — removes all local data from your device.
2. **Remove iCloud data** — go to Settings → *your name* → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Planner → Delete Data.

Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered by the developer as we do not hold copies of it.
