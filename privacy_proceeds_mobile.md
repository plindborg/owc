# Proceeds Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

[Support](./support.md)

Proceeds is a native Apple platform app for reviewing revenue, subscriptions, markets, reports, and timeline activity for app businesses. We design the app to keep your workspace data local to your device whenever possible.

Replace the example contact details on this page before publishing it publicly or linking it in App Store Connect.

## Summary

Proceeds does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or cross-app tracking. The app only accesses business data that you import yourself or sync using features you explicitly enable.

## Information You Provide

You may choose to provide:

- Local CSV, JSON, TXT, TSV, or compatible report files that you import manually
- App Store Connect API credentials that you enter yourself
- App Store Connect report data retrieved using your own credentials
- App preferences such as language, theme, currency, and workspace settings

## How We Use Information

Information is used only to operate app features that you request, including:

- Displaying dashboards, tables, charts, and founder reports
- Generating exports that you explicitly create
- Syncing App Store Connect data when you choose to connect your account
- Converting amounts using exchange-rate data when needed
- Showing widgets using locally shared app data on your device

## Where Information Is Stored

- Imported and synced workspace data is stored locally on your device
- App preferences are stored locally using Apple platform storage APIs
- App Store Connect private keys are stored locally in the Apple keychain on supported platforms
- Widget summaries may be stored in local shared app-group storage on your device

## Network Use

Proceeds uses network access only for features you choose to use, such as:

- App Store Connect sync
- Exchange-rate retrieval for currency conversion
- Apple platform services needed for purchases, subscriptions, widgets, or related system behavior

## Tracking and Data Sharing

Proceeds does not track you across apps or websites, does not sell your data, and does not use your imported workspace data for advertising.

Your data may be sent only to services required for features you actively use, such as Apple services for App Store Connect, StoreKit, widgets, or system account handling, and exchange-rate providers used for currency conversion.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Use demo data only when you explicitly enable it
- Remove stored App Store Connect credentials from Settings
- Restore or manage subscriptions through Apple system flows

## Children

Proceeds is intended for business and productivity use and is not directed to children.
