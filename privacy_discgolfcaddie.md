# Discgolf Smart Caddie Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This is a draft and should be reviewed before publication.

## English draft

### Privacy Policy for Discgolf Smart Caddie

Discgolf Smart Caddie ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is handled, and what choices you have.

### 1. Information you create in the app

When you use Discgolf Smart Caddie, you may create or store information such as:

- player profile details
- disc bag information
- courses and hole data
- round scores and notes
- shot logs, practice sessions, and statistics

This information is primarily stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share content.

### 2. Permissions the app may request

#### Location

The app may request access to your location to:

- show current wind conditions
- improve weather-based recommendations
- use course-aware location context

Location access is optional.

#### Camera

The app may request access to your camera when you choose to scan disc flight numbers from a disc photo.

Camera access is optional.

#### Photo Library

The app may request access to your photo library when you choose an image to scan disc flight numbers.

Photo library access is optional.

### 3. Weather and map services

If you use weather or map-related features, relevant information such as approximate location or course coordinates may be sent to Apple services, including WeatherKit or MapKit, in order to return weather data or map content.

### 4. How we use information

We use information in the app to:

- power scorekeeping and bag management
- generate caddie recommendations
- calculate round statistics and training insights
- improve the in-app coaching experience
- support sharing and export features when you choose to use them

### 5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your app data with advertisers.

Data may be processed by Apple services only when required to deliver Apple-powered features such as weather or maps.

### 6. Data retention

Data you create in the app remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or remove the app.

If you use export or share features, any exported content is handled according to the destination you choose.

### 7. Your choices

You can:

- deny location access
- deny camera access
- deny photo library access
- delete app data by removing the app or clearing data you created

You can also manage permissions later in the iPhone Settings app.

### 8. Children's privacy

Discgolf Smart Caddie is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.

### 10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: offworldcolonies73@gmail.com
