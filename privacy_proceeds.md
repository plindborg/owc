# Privacy Policy for Proceeds

_Last updated: 2026-03-29_

## Overview
Proceeds is a native macOS app for reviewing app revenue, subscriptions, markets, reports, and related analytics.

This app is designed to minimize data collection. We do not run advertising, third-party analytics, or user tracking inside the app.

## Data You Provide
Proceeds may process data that you choose to import or connect, including:
- local CSV, JSON, or supported report files that you import manually
- optional App Store Connect credentials that you enter yourself
- optional App Store Connect report data retrieved using your credentials

## How Data Is Used
Your data is used only to:
- display analytics and reports inside the app
- generate exports that you explicitly request
- power widgets through local app-group storage on your device
- convert currencies using exchange-rate lookups when needed

## Storage
- Imported data is stored locally on your device.
- The App Store Connect private key is stored in the macOS keychain.
- App preferences are stored locally using Apple platform storage APIs.

## Network Access
Proceeds uses the network only for:
- optional App Store Connect sync that you initiate or enable
- exchange-rate retrieval for currency conversion

## Tracking
Proceeds does not track you across apps or websites.

## Sharing
We do not sell your data. We do not share your imported business data with third parties except when your device requests Apple platform services or external APIs required for features you use, such as App Store Connect sync or exchange-rate retrieval.

## Your Choices
You can:
- avoid connecting App Store Connect entirely
- clear imported data from the app at any time
- disable demo data and widgets
- remove stored App Store Connect credentials from Settings
